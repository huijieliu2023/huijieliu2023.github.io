Biography

Huijie Liu is an Assistant Professor at Shenzhen University. She received her Ph.D. from the University of Science and Technology of China. Her research focuses on data mining, graph neural networks, knowledge relation modeling, technological knowledge mining, and multimodal large models.

Research Interests

  • Graph Neural Networks
  • Technological Knowledge Flow
  • Knowledge Combination Modeling
  • Data Mining and Knowledge Discovery
  • Multimodal AI
