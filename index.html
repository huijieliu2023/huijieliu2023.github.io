Biography

I am currently an Assistant Professor with the College of Computer Science and Software Engineering, Shenzhen University, and a member of the Visual Computing Research Center (VCC) led by Prof. Hui Huang. I received my Ph.D. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China, supervised by Prof. Qi Liu and Prof. Enhong Chen.

My research interests lie in data mining, graph neural networks, technological knowledge mining, and multimodal large language models. I am particularly interested in developing graph-structured and large-model-enhanced methods for modeling complex relations, cross-modal understanding, and real-world intelligent applications.

Research Interests

Graph Learning and Data Mining

Graph neural networks, heterophilic graph learning, graph representation learning, and knowledge discovery.
